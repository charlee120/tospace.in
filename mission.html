Our Missions

PROBE - 1

The mission launch is named PROBE 1- (Pravaha Radio-module Operations Balloon Experiment). Pravaha in Sanskrit means "Stratosphere" which indicates the altitude of our test launch.


It is an Indo-Italian collaboration with Deltasapce, Italy. We are launching a helium balloon to 1Lahk feet to test our Satellite sub-systems. It contains communication modules, antennas and other sensor payloads in preparation for deploying our satellite into orbit.


We are glad that we have been recognized by Pradhan Mandhri Start-up Yojana (Startup India). As a part of giving back our love and gratitude to the nation, we have planned to send our National Flag and a photograph of our Honourable Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Honourable Cheif Minister of Tamil Nadu M K Stalin along with our subsystem also a note of தமிழ் வாழ்க(Tamil Long live).


It will reach 1 lakh feet altitude and 1.38 billion people's hearts.

CURRENT MISSION

Designing and assembling in Tospace Headquarters and with the help of the Collaborators and the launch is secured with the Fossa systems in SpaceX, weighing 250 g and measuring 5 cm on all sides.


It was the first picosatellite built and put into the orbit of India. This Open Source satellite communicates IoT data with Earth, the free global Communications through the use of low-power RF modules, compatible with the latest IoT technology.


And the Main utility of the picosatellite is to provide a very secure and dedicated IoT connectivity using LoRa Gateways as a Payload and becoming the demonstrator of the Tospace architecture.

FUTURE MISSION

The technology that we are developing is a new paradigm in LoT(LoRa of Things) communications. It is a low-power IoT module, that can serve as an independent node to create and maintain networks. With our Lora Gateways, this technology can be used to create networks and connect remote areas. We believe that we can help bridge the digital divide by connecting remote areas and empowering the underserved populations around the world.
